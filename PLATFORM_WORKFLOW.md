# CivicReport Platform Workflow

## üì± User Journey: From Issue to Resolution

### Step 1: Citizen Reports Issue
**User Action:**
- Opens CivicReport app/website
- Clicks "Report an Issue" button
- Takes photo or video of the civic problem
- App automatically captures GPS location

**Examples of Issues:**
- Potholes on roads
- Broken streetlights
- Garbage accumulation
- Water supply problems
- Drainage issues
- Park maintenance
- Public property damage
- Traffic signal malfunction

---

### Step 2: AI Analysis & Classification
**Automated Process:**
- AI analyzes the uploaded photo/video
- Identifies the type of civic issue
- Categorizes into 15+ predefined categories:
  - Roads & Infrastructure
  - Water Supply
  - Drainage & Sewage
  - Waste Management
  - Street Lighting
  - Parks & Recreation
  - Public Health
  - Traffic Management
  - Building Violations
  - Noise Pollution
  - And more...

**AI Capabilities:**
- Image recognition for issue type
- Severity assessment
- Location verification
- Duplicate detection
- Priority assignment

---

### Step 3: Smart Routing to Authority
**Automated Routing:**
Based on location and issue type, the report is sent to:

#### For Kozhikode Corporation Areas:
- Corporation Ward Office
- Specific department (Roads, Water, Sanitation, etc.)
- Ward Councillor

#### For Municipalities:
- Vadakara Municipality
- Koyilandy Municipality
- Relevant municipal department

#### For Panchayats:
- Gram Panchayat Office
- Block Panchayat (if needed)
- District Panchayat (for major issues)

**Notification Sent To:**
- Concerned department head
- Ward representative
- Maintenance team
- Citizen (confirmation)

---

### Step 4: Authority Action & Updates
**Government Side:**
- Receives notification with:
  - Issue details
  - Photo/video evidence
  - Exact location (GPS coordinates)
  - Citizen contact (optional)
  - AI-suggested priority level

**Action Workflow:**
1. **Acknowledgment** (within 2 hours)
   - Issue logged in system
   - Citizen receives confirmation

2. **Assignment** (within 24 hours)
   - Assigned to field team
   - Timeline set based on priority

3. **In Progress** (work begins)
   - Field team dispatched
   - Updates posted to system
   - Citizen can track progress

4. **Resolution** (work completed)
   - Photos of completed work uploaded
   - Citizen receives notification
   - Feedback requested

---

- Citizen receives notification of completion

  - Not resolved within SLA time
